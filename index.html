Physics Directed Reading Program (DRP) at UGA

We pair curious undergraduate students with mentors to study a special topic of their choice. Near the end of the semester, our students get to present their favorite topic from their directed readings at the Physics DRP Presentation Night.

Benefits for Mentees

  • Experience studying outside the classroom
  • Presentation experience
  • Possible future research projects
  • Specialization in a particular topic
  • CV item for grad school, REUs, scholarships, or fellowships

Benefits for Mentors

  • Mentorship and advising experience
  • Teaching a special topic of your interest
  • Building stronger connections in the physics community at our department
